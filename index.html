Giorgia Pitteri

I am a third year PhD student in Computer Vision and Deep Learning in the Imagine Team of the Ecole des Ponts Paristech, Paris.

I'm working on 3D Object Pose Estimation under the supervision of Vincent Lepetit and my project is funded by Siemens AG Research & Technology Center, Munich.

I received a master degree in Control Engineering and a bachelor degree in Information Engineering from University of Padova, Italy in 2015 and 2013 respectively.

Before starting the PhD, I spent two years in industry working as Control Algorithms Engineer and Research Engineer in Computer Vision.
